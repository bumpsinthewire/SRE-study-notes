## Key Concepts

## Questions & Notes

Subnets:

- reside within a single Availability Zone (AZ)
- can be either public or private
- must be within /16 to /28
- first 4 and last IPs are reserved
  - network address
  - VPC router
  - DNS
  - future use
  - broadcast
